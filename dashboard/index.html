    📍 Data Enrichment

    Fill in missing geo data (metro distance, walk score, POIs, OMI zones) for every city's listings. One click queues every city × rentals + sales.

    ⚖️ Scoring Parameters

    Tune the composite score formula. Changes save to scoring_settings.json and apply on the next scan. Weights must sum to 1.0. Rescore now re-runs scoring on cached data immediately.

    🏷️ Badge Thresholds

    Minimum sub-scores a listing must hit on every gate simultaneously to earn a badge. Lowering thresholds adds more badges; raising them tightens qualification. Hit Save & Rescore to apply immediately.
